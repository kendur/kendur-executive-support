# Research

This folder contains background research, references, and notes that inform the design of the Executive Support System.

---

## Purpose

Research notes here support design decisions, capture sources of inspiration, and document what has been learned from studying comparable systems, methodologies, and tools.

Research notes are informal. They may be incomplete, opinionated, or exploratory. They are not specifications.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Notes on existing productivity systems (GTD, PARA, Zettelkasten, etc.) and how they do or don't apply
- Tool evaluations (Notion, Obsidian, Roam, etc.)
- Notes on capture hardware and services (voice recorders, transcription tools)
- Automation platform comparisons
- AI integration approaches
- User behaviour patterns relevant to the system design
- Links to external articles, papers, or posts that informed a decision

---

## Structure

Each research file should have:

- A short title
- A date or date range
- A brief description of what was studied
- Key findings or takeaways
- Links or references where applicable

There is no required template. Notes here are meant to be useful, not formal.
