# ADR-0003: Open Community Core and Paid Services

**Date:** 2026-07-22  
**Status:** Accepted

## Context

The project aims to help people with similar problems while supporting sustainable development and maintenance.

## Decision

The complete manual community system will be free and openly licensed. Users may fork, modify, redistribute, and publish derivatives under attribution terms. Future paid offerings provide hosting, managed automation, optional intelligence, migration, monitoring, testing, and support.

## Alternatives considered

- Sell the template as a closed one-time product.
- Gate core workflows behind a subscription.
- Provide the project without a sustainable service path.

## Consequences

- Licensing must preserve attribution and the canonical-source link.
- Official tested releases remain distinguishable from forks.
- Paid value comes from reduced technical and maintenance burden rather than withholding the core workflow.
