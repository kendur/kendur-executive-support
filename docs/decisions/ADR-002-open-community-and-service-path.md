# ADR-002: Open community edition with service-based paid path

**Date:** 2026-07-22
**Status:** Accepted

## Context

The repository needs a clear product model that balances open community access with future commercial
sustainability.

## Options Considered

- Keep the entire project closed until monetisation is defined
- Publish a free Community edition and charge for the core workflow later
- Keep the core Community edition open and build paid offerings around services and hosting

## Decision

Keep the core Community edition free, openly licensed, forkable, and publicly updated while maintained. If
paid offerings are added, they should focus on hosted automation, managed support, and later Platform or
Enterprise tracks.

## Reasoning

This preserves trust and portability for the core workflow while leaving room for sustainable value-added
services.

## Consequences

Derivatives must preserve attribution and link back to the canonical source. Official branding and
maintainer-tested release status remain separate from general reuse rights.
